# Human writing reference (hub)

Avoid **clusters** of tells, not single words alone.

## Structure tells

Bold on every term; in-body section headers; numbered question lists; rule-of-three bullets; "In conclusion"; causal em dashes (`— so`, `— meaning`); staccato fragments; "Here's the thing:" openers.

## Contrastive negation (rewrite)

"It's not X, it's Y" → state what it is.

## Vocabulary clusters (ban when 3+)

delve, leverage, robust, comprehensive, seamless, pivotal, landscape (abstract), underscore, foster, navigate, transformative, synergy, deep dive, at the end of the day, moreover, furthermore, in conclusion, moving forward, unpack, elevate, streamline, harness.

## Human signals

Mix short and long sentences; one specific name/date/number; one main point first; optional single hedge ("probably"); vary paragraph length.

## German LLM-Deutsch (if DE)

im Rahmen von, darüber hinaus, zusammenfassend lässt sich sagen, im Zuge der Digitalisierung, grundsätzlich lässt sich festhalten.
